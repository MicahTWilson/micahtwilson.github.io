Skip Checkout

A mobile checkout solution for your local grocery store

New

Skip is a self-checkout app that allows you to skip the checkout line at your local grocery store, pharmacy, or convenience store. Scan all your items as you shop, then leave the store without ever waiting in a line.

Skip is free to download and use. If Skip is not available at your local store, please tell the store manager you want Skip and we’d be happy to work with them.
